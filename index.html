What is a robot?

“ Any automatically operated machine that replaces human effort, though it may not resemble human beings in appearance or perform functions in a humanlike manner. ”

Encyclopaedia Britannica

“ A mechanical device that sometimes resembles a human and is capable of performing a variety of often complex human tasks on command or by being programmed in advance. ”

The Free Dictionary

a machine capable of carrying out a complex series of actions automatically, especially one programmable by a computer half of all American robots are making cars or trucks (oxford dictionary)
